Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use Spendly, we store the expense records you create, including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide. We do not collect payment information or any sensitive financial account data.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service — displaying your expense history, generating summaries, and enabling the features you use. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app for your benefit.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your information from unauthorised access. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly uses Google Fonts to load typefaces used in the interface. No personal data is shared with third parties for marketing or analytics purposes. If this changes, we will update this policy and notify users in advance.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us at privacy@spendly.app. We aim to respond to all enquiries within 48 hours.
